I received my doctoral degree in computer science under the supervision of [Eric Bodden](https://bodden.de) at Paderborn University. My doctoral research focused on scaling precise static code analyses on large-scale code bases, mainly through sparse data-flow and pointer analyses. I lead the development of the [SootUp](https://github.com/soot-oss/SootUp) static analysis framework.
